Welcome to the MineKhan Launcher, the easiest way to play just about any version of MineKhan at a moment's notice, with or without internet! This project, begun in 2022, was initially made available to the public in late 2023. You are currently using a work-in-progress build of v0.7; last previous update v0.6.4, released 7/05/2025.

Shortcut Setup (Optional):
Right-click on the background of your computer. Select "New > New Shortcut." Once created, click "Properties" and set the image to the favicon.ico file. Title the shorcut "MineKhan Launcher [VersionNumber]"

Current location of favicon.ico in MKL v0.7: [ParentFolder]\MineKhanLauncher-v0.7-indev\Files\Assets\favicon.ico)
Location of favicon.ico in MKL v0.6/V6: [ParentFolder]\MineKhanLauncherV[no.]\Files\Assets\favicon.ico)

Location of favicon.ico in MKL V1: [ParentFolder]\MineKhanLauncherV1\favicon.ico
